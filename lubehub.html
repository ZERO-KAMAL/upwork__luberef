Finished Lubricants Blending

  • Construction of a blending plant to produce finished lubricants for use in a variety of automotive and industrial applications.
  • Blended lubricants are prepared by blending base oils with a variety of additives depending on what the final application will be.
  • Luberef Yanbu produces Group II base oils and bright stock that can be used to produce a wide variety of lubricants.
  • Two major additive blenders are located within the Yanbu ecosystem.

What is LubeHub

A strategically located hub of excellence, Lubehub brings together multiple Speciality Products Producesrs and operators associated with the base oil industry and encourages overseas blenders to open facilities in Saudi Arabia to directly benefit from reduced shipping delays and costs, receiving high quality product directly from Yanbu.

The development is a key contributor to Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 in attracting inward investment opportunities and offers financing, trading, and employment incentives.
